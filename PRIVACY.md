# Privacy Policy — Cardamom

_Last updated: June 13, 2026_

Cardamom is designed to do its work entirely inside your browser. It does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

## What the extension accesses

When you click the Cardamom icon on a web page, the extension reads the content of **that page only**, at that moment, to find recipe information (the dish title, photo, ingredients, instructions, and source). This uses Chrome's `activeTab` permission, which grants access to a page only in response to your click.

## What the extension does with it

The recipe information is used solely to render the card you see in the popup, and — only if you choose — to copy text to your clipboard or generate an image you download. All of this happens locally on your device.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** send any page content, recipe data, or browsing activity to any server.
- It does **not** use analytics, tracking, cookies, or advertising.
- It does **not** collect personal or identifying information.
- It does **not** run in the background or read pages you haven't clicked the icon on.

## Network requests

The only network activity is your browser loading the recipe's own photo (from the recipe website's image URL) so it can be shown on the card. No data is sent anywhere by the extension.
